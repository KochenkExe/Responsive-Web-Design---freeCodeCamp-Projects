Ki Hajar Dewantara

The Father of Indonesian National Education

A timeline of Ki Hajar Dewantara's life

  • 2 May 1889

    Born in Yogyakarta, Dutch East Indies, as Raden Mas Soewardi Soerjaningrat.

  • 1896

    Starts his basic education in Europeesche. Lagere School

  • 1904

    Continues his education at the Kweekschool, a teacher education school.

  • 1905

    Receives a scholarship to study in the School tot Opleiding van Inlandsche Artsen, the medical school in Batavia.

  • 1907

    Marries with RA Soetartinah.

  • 1908

    Starts his active role in Boedi Oetomo in their propaganda service.

  • 1912

    Appoints as the head of Sarekat Islam Bandung Branch

  • 1912

    Establishes Indische Partij with Douwes Dekker and dr Tjipto Mangoenkoesoemo

  • 13 July 1913

    Writes Als ik eens Nederlander was (If I were a Dutchman) which critizes the colonial government of the Dutch East Indies.

  • 6 September 1913

    Exiles to the Netherlands, along with his wife and the Tiga Serangkai.

  • 1915

    Finishes his education and received the European certificate.

  • 1919

    Returns home to Java with his wife.

  • 1922

    Establishes the Taman Siswa school in Yogyakarta, a Javanese educational movement that strove to provide education for the native population.

  • 1945

    Appoints as the Indonesian Minister of Education and Culture

  • 26 April 1959

    Dies in Yogyakarta and buried in Taman Wijaya Brata cemetery.
